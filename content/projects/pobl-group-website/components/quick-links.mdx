#### Fields:
- Items (repeatable)
    - Title - the main heading of the card
    - Description - a short paragraph placed under the title
    - Button - a button that redirects users to certain page/external website

> By default, the background colour is set to white. To change it, <b>modify the colour of the button</b>. The card will change its background colour based on the colour you pick for the button. <br/> Also, the text colour will be adjusted automatically based on the brightness of the background colour.
