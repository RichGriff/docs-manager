General Setting

#### Fields:
- Title - the main title shown before all location cards
- Location Cards (repeatable) - a list of cards containing info about each location

Location Card Setting (Repeatable)

<img src='https://files.gitbook.com/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2Fqq1gvL7j0PwJkFX9MJae%2Fuploads%2FP4F3ZTU1N5hppeE42vQm%2FScreenshot%202023-10-25%20at%2015.31.27.png?alt=media&token=202a23a4-f74b-4290-9cd2-fc4d3c4472cf' />

#### Fields:
- Heading (required) - the main title of the card
- Description - the short paragraph underneath the heading
- Image (required) - the main image of the card
- Alt_text (required) - the alternative text of the image for accessibility or when the image cannot be displayed for some reason.
- Button - shows a call-to-action button with texts and a URL
