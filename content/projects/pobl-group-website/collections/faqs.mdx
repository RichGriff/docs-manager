#### Fields:
- Category - groups of FAQs, determines in which section/page this FAQ shows up (see the options below)
- Question - the title of this FAQ
- Answer - the content 
- Button - an optional button to add for redirection

Options of `Category`
- Repairs - on /report-a-repair
- Rent Charges - on /make-a-payment/rent-charges
- Service Charges - on /make-a-payment/service-charges 
- Application & guidance - on /pobl-trust/application-and-guidance
- ABS (Anti-social Behaviour) - on /anti-social-behaviour
- Cost of Living - on /cost-of-living

<Callout>
Currently, the FAQs for Repairs and Rent Charges are not implemented on the page.
</Callout>
