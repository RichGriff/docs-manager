#### Fields:
- Name - name of the person
- Avatar - an image of the person
- Email - contact email
- Linkedin - link for Linkedin
- Type - which group this person belongs to (see Options for Type below)
- Role - the job title of the person
- Details - where you put their personal statement, and it's what will be shown in modal

Options for Type
- Executive
- Pobl Board
- Pobl Trust
- NHM
