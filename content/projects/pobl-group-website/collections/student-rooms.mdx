#### Fields:
- Name - the title shown on the room card
- Description - a short paragraph describing the room
- Gallery - shows images of the room, multiple images supported
- Type (required) - categorises the room into one of the 4 types (see Options for Room Types) 
- Availability - the number of available room left
- Price 39/43/48/50 - the price of this room for 39, 43, 48 and 50 weeks respectively

Options for Room Types
- Budget
- Essential
- Premium
- Deluxe
